[[傅立葉轉換]] 在[[卷積]]計算時，擁有以下特性
$$ (f \star g)(t)  \Longleftrightarrow (H \cdot G)(u)$$

#### 證明
$$\begin{align} 

\mathcal{F}\{(f \star h)(t)\} &= \int_{-\infty}^\infty \left[ \int_{-\infty}^\infty f(\tau) h(t - \tau) d\tau \right] e^{-j 2 \pi \mu t} dt \\

&= \int_{-\infty}^\infty f(\tau) \left[ \int_{-\infty}^\infty h(t - \tau) e^{-j 2 \pi \mu t} dt \right] d\tau \\

&= \int^{\infty}_{-\infty} f(\tau) \left[H(u)e^{-j2 \pi u \tau} \right]d\tau \\

&=( H \cdot F)(u)

\end{align}$$

$$ 
\begin{align}
\int^{\infty}_{-\infty}h(t - \tau) e^{-j2 \pi  \mu t} dt &= \int^{\infty}_{-\infty} h(v) e^{-j2 \pi \mu (v + \tau)} dv \\
&= e^{-j2 \pi \mu \tau} \int^{\infty}_{-\infty}h(v)e^{-j2 \pi \mu v} dv\\
&= e^{-j2 \pi \tau} H(v)
\end{align}
$$
